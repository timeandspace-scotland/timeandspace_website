Time and Space

What's time and space?

Time and Space is a unique service in Glasgow offering support and information to people who hear voices and people who self-harm and their supporters. We believe that it is possible to find a way to manage and live with your experiences.

What are our aims?

To raise awareness of voice hearing, visions, tactile sensations and other sensory experiences and self harm. To give men women and children who have these experiences an opportunity to talk freely about this together. To support anyone with these experiences seeking to understand, learn and grow from them in their own way. To inform and support the families of those who hear voices and those who self harm.

How do we achieve our aims?

Promoting, developing and supporting our client led support groups. Organizing and delivering training sessions for our workers and the general public. Making available information and support to people who experience hearing voices, seeing visions and tactile sensations and those with experience of self harm. Making available information and support to family members who are supporting voice hearers or those who self harm. To give men, women and children who have these experiences an opportunity to talk freely about them. Produce two newsletters a year.
